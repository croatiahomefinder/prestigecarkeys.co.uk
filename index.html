CAN Phantom immobiliser

Modern thieves can access vehicles without touching the original key. CAN Phantom adds an invisible layer of security by preventing the car from starting unless a secret button sequence is entered.

Key features

  • Stops the engine running without your unique PIN sequence.
  • PIN entered via standard vehicle buttons (e.g. steering wheel).
  • No extra key fobs, tags or keypads to carry.
  • Suitable for many Porsche, Volvo & JLR models.
  • Valet and service modes for workshops and handovers.

Best time to install

The most cost-effective time to fit CAN Phantom is during key programming, as we’re already connected to the vehicle systems. That’s why the price drops to £399 when installed at the same time as your car key work.
